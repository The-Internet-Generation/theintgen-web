How TigGigs works.

Five steps, brief to paid. Click any step for the full picture.

1 Brief
comes in
add

A client fills our 3-step form on the Post a Gig page — who they are, what they need (design, edit or shoot), the deadline, and any references. Takes under 5 minutes.

2 TIG reviews
& lists it
add

Our ops team checks the brief is clear, sets a fair rate off the rate card, and the job goes live on the board — usually within one business day.

3 You
claim it
add

Everyone can browse the board, but only verified TIG freelancers can claim. Unlock with your registered email, then it's first-come, first-served — no bidding.

4 Work gets
submitted
add

You drop the finished files in the shared submission folder before the deadline. Status flips to "Submitted" and our ops team gets notified to review.

5 Reviewed
& paid
add

Ops or the client reviews the delivery. Approved work gets posted and you're paid out — if revisions are needed, it loops back to step 4.
